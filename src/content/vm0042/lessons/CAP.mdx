    <h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-800 mb-3">Answer Guide, Deliverable 8 (ERR Calculation)</h3>
    <p class="text-sm text-gray-500 mb-3">This is the only deliverable with a worked answer. Use it to check your calculation method.</p>
    <FormulaBox>
      <strong>Step 1: SOC sequestration</strong><br/>
      Vertisols: 4,400 ha x 0.35 tC/ha/yr x 3.67 = <strong>5,649 tCO2e/yr</strong><br/>
      Alfisols: 3,600 ha x 0.25 tC/ha/yr x 3.67 = <strong>3,303 tCO2e/yr</strong><br/>
      SOC subtotal: 5,649 + 3,303 = <strong>8,952 tCO2e/yr</strong><br/><br/>
      <strong>Step 2: N2O reduction</strong><br/>
      8,000 ha x 0.05 tCO2e/ha/yr = <strong>400 tCO2e/yr</strong><br/><br/>
      <strong>Step 3: Stubble burning elimination</strong><br/>
      8,000 ha x 0.15 tCO2e/ha/yr = <strong>1,200 tCO2e/yr</strong><br/><br/>
      <strong>Step 4: Gross ERRs</strong><br/>
      8,952 + 400 + 1,200 = <strong>10,552 tCO2e/yr</strong><br/><br/>
      <strong>Step 5: Leakage deduction (5%)</strong><br/>
      10,552 x 0.05 = 528 tCO2e<br/>
      Net ERRs = 10,552 - 528 = <strong>10,024 tCO2e/yr</strong>
