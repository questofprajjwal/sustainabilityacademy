SFDR 2.0 RESEARCH SOURCES INDEX
================================

Purpose: Reference material for writing SFDR 2.0 lesson content

OFFICIAL DOCUMENTS (PDFs)
--------------------------

1. EC_COM_2025_841_SFDR2_Proposal.pdf (564 KB)
   Title: European Commission SFDR 2.0 Legislative Proposal
   Reference: COM(2025) 841 final
   Date: 20 November 2025
   Source: EUR-Lex (https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/PDF/?uri=CELEX:52025PC0841)
   Content: The formal legislative proposal for SFDR revision. 62 pages covering all Articles, recitals,
   and explanatory memorandum. Defines the three new product categories (Articles 7, 8, 9), abolishes
   entity-level PAI statements, introduces 70% investment thresholds, mandatory exclusions, and 2-page
   disclosure templates.

2. ESAs_Joint_Opinion_JC_2024_06.pdf (484 KB)
   Title: Joint ESAs Opinion on the Assessment of the Sustainable Finance Disclosure Regulation
   Reference: JC 2024 06
   Date: 18 June 2024
   Source: ESMA (https://www.esma.europa.eu/sites/default/files/2024-06/JC_2024_06_Joint_ESAs_Opinion_on_SFDR.pdf)
   Content: EBA, EIOPA, and ESMA's joint formal opinion recommending SFDR reform. Proposes replacing
   Articles 8/9 with two product categories (Sustainability + Transition) and/or sustainability indicator
   grades. Key precursor document to the November 2025 EC proposal.

3. ESAs_PAI_Disclosures_Report_JC_2025_26.pdf (776 KB)
   Title: Report on Principal Adverse Impact Disclosures under Article 18 SFDR
   Reference: JC 2025 26
   Date: September 2025
   Source: ESMA (https://www.esma.europa.eu/sites/default/files/2025-09/JC_2025_26_Report_on_PAI_disclosures_under_Article_18_SFDR.pdf)
   Content: Empirical assessment of how PAI disclosures have functioned under SFDR 1.0 in practice.
   Provides evidence base supporting the proposal to abolish entity-level PAI statements and streamline
   product-level PAI requirements.

4. EC_2023_SFDR_Targeted_Consultation.pdf (480 KB)
   Title: SFDR Implementation - Targeted Consultation Document
   Date: 14 September 2023
   Source: European Commission DG FISMA
   (https://finance.ec.europa.eu/system/files/2023-09/2023-sfdr-implementation-targeted-consultation-document_en.pdf)
   Content: The formal consultation document setting out the review questions on SFDR effectiveness,
   interaction with other regulations, potential categorization system, and disclosure improvements.

5. EC_2023_SFDR_Consultation_Summary_Report.pdf (772 KB)
   Title: Summary Report of the Open and Targeted Consultations on the SFDR Assessment
   Date: 3 May 2024
   Source: European Commission DG FISMA
   Content: Compiled results from the September-December 2023 consultations. Documents that 89% of
   respondents found disclosure objectives still relevant, 94% preferred EU-level rules, and 77%
   highlighted lack of legal clarity and data availability problems.

KEY THEMES FOR LESSON WRITING
-------------------------------
1. The "de facto label" problem: How the market informally treated Articles 8/9 as labels
2. The fundamental paradigm shift: From disclosure framework to mandatory categorization regime
3. The three new categories: Article 7 (Transition), Article 8 (ESG Basics), Article 9 (Sustainable)
4. The 70% rule: Minimum investment threshold for all three categories
5. Mandatory exclusions: Tiered strictness across the three categories
6. Removal of "sustainable investment" concept and associated DNSH/good governance tests
7. Entity-level PAI abolition and product-level PAI changes
8. Disclosure simplification: 2-page templates, abolition of Article 10 website disclosures
9. Naming and marketing restrictions: Non-categorized funds lose ESG terminology rights
10. Implementation timeline: Proposal November 2025 -> Application late 2028 at earliest
11. Industry impact: Many current Article 8 funds expected to downgrade to non-categorized
12. Remaining uncertainties: Delegated acts still to be finalized; unresolved policy debates
