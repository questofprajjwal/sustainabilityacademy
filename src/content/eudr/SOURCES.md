## Module-by-Module Coverage

### Module 0: Deforestation and Global Supply Chains
- EUDR Regulation (EU) 2023/1115 - Recitals 1-28 (context and rationale)
- FAO Global Forest Resources Assessment 2020 (deforestation statistics)
- EC EUDR Impact Assessment SWD(2021) 315 (EU consumption footprint analysis)
- EC EUDR FAQ (policy evolution and voluntary pledge background)
- EU Timber Regulation (EU) 995/2010 (predecessor legislation comparison)

### Module 1: Scope and Definitions
- EUDR Regulation (EU) 2023/1115 - Art. 1-3 and Annex I (commodity scope and CN codes)
- FAO Global Forest Resources Assessment 2020 (FAO forest definition underpinning Art. 2)
- EC EUDR Guidance Documents (derived products, operator/trader distinction)
- EC EUDR FAQ (SME provisions, online marketplace obligations, FPIC)

### Module 2: The Due Diligence System
- EUDR Regulation (EU) 2023/1115 - Art. 4-11, 33 (due diligence system and information system)
- EC Implementing Regulation on geolocation data requirements
- EC Implementing Regulations on benchmarking (simplified due diligence triggers)
- EC EUDR Guidance Documents (practical DDS guidance)
- EC EUDR FAQ (reference numbers, responsibility chain)

### Module 3: The Benchmarking System
- EUDR Regulation (EU) 2023/1115 - Art. 29-32 (benchmarking framework and country partnerships)
- EC Implementing Regulations on benchmarking (assessment criteria and data sources)
- EC EUDR FAQ (risk category implications and review process)
- EC EUDR Guidance Documents (sub-national benchmarking approach)

### Module 4: Implementation Challenges
- EUDR Regulation (EU) 2023/1115 - Art. 9-10, 21, Annex I, Recitals 53-58
- EC EUDR Guidance Documents (traceability system requirements)
- Global Forest Watch data (satellite monitoring capabilities)
- EC EUDR FAQ (smallholder provisions and collective compliance)

### Module 5: Enforcement, Compliance, and Future Outlook
- EUDR Regulation (EU) 2023/1115 - Art. 14-26, 29, 36-38 (enforcement and timeline)
- EC EUDR FAQ (penalty structure, check rates, transition provisions)
- CSDDD Directive (EU) 2024/1760 (interplay with supply chain due diligence)
- CSRD Directive (EU) 2022/2464 (deforestation disclosures under ESRS)
- UK Environment Act 2021, Schedule 17 (forest risk commodities provisions)
- EC EUDR Guidance Documents (certification scheme recognition)
