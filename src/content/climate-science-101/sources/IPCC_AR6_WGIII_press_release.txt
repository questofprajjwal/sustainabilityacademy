IPCC AR6 Working Group III Press Release
"Climate Change 2022: Mitigation of Climate Change"
Released: April 4, 2022
Source: https://www.ipcc.ch/2022/04/04/ipcc-ar6-wgiii-pressrelease/

MAIN HEADLINE: "The evidence is clear: the time for action is now. We can halve emissions by 2030."

CORE FINDINGS:

Emissions Status:
Global greenhouse gas emissions in 2010-2019 reached their highest levels ever, though growth rate has slowed. Without immediate, deep reductions across sectors, limiting warming to 1.5°C becomes impossible.

Cost Reductions Since 2010:
Solar and wind energy costs, plus battery technology, have dropped up to 85%.

CRITICAL TIMELINES:

For 1.5°C warming limit:
- Emissions must peak before 2025
- Reduce by 43% by 2030
- Methane cut by approximately one-third
- Achieve net-zero CO2 in early 2050s

For 2°C warming limit:
- Emissions peak before 2025
- Reduce by 25% by 2030
- Net-zero CO2 in early 2070s

SECTORAL OPPORTUNITIES:

Energy: Requires substantial fossil fuel reduction, electrification, efficiency improvements, and hydrogen alternatives.

Buildings: Zero-energy and zero-carbon structures exist across all climates; action this decade is critical.

Industry: Enhanced material efficiency, reuse, recycling; accounts for approximately 25% of global emissions.

Land Use: Agriculture and forestry offer large-scale reduction and carbon storage potential.

INVESTMENT GAP:
Financial flows are 3-6 times below needed 2030 levels, yet sufficient global capital exists if governments provide clear signals.
