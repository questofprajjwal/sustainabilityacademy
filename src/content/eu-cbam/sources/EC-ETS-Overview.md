# EU Emissions Trading System (EU ETS)
![](https://climate.ec.europa.eu/sites/default/files/styles/oe_theme_full_width_banner_4_1/public/2024-11/banner002c.jpg.webp?h=15331361&itok=HBd0Gypw)
© 2025 Getty Images
EU ETS
EU Emissions Trading System
[About the EU ETS](https://climate.ec.europa.eu/eu-action/carbon-markets/about-eu-ets_en)
Learn more about the basics of the EU ETS and its functioning.
[Scope of the EU ETS](https://climate.ec.europa.eu/eu-action/carbon-markets/eu-emissions-trading-system-eu-ets/scope-eu-ets_en)
What countries, sectors and gases does the EU ETS cover?
[Market Stability Reserve](https://climate.ec.europa.eu/eu-action/carbon-markets/eu-emissions-trading-system-eu-ets/market-stability-reserve_en)
Improving our resilience to major shocks.
[EU ETS emissions cap](https://climate.ec.europa.eu/eu-action/carbon-markets/eu-emissions-trading-system-eu-ets/eu-ets-emissions-cap_en)
Limiting emissions year after year.
[Auctioning of allowances](https://climate.ec.europa.eu/eu-action/carbon-markets/eu-emissions-trading-system-eu-ets/auctioning-allowances_en)
Putting into practice the ‘polluter pays’ principle.
[Use of international credits](https://climate.ec.europa.eu/eu-action/carbon-markets/eu-emissions-trading-system-eu-ets/use-international-credits_en)
Financial instruments that represent a tonne of CO₂ removed or reduced from the atmosphere.
[Free allocation](https://climate.ec.europa.eu/eu-action/carbon-markets/eu-emissions-trading-system-eu-ets/free-allocation_en)
Mitigating the risk of carbon leakage.
[Monitoring, reporting and verification](https://climate.ec.europa.eu/eu-action/carbon-markets/eu-emissions-trading-system-eu-ets/monitoring-reporting-and-verification_en)
Ensuring transparent and accurate emissions trading under the EU ETS.
[Union Registry](https://climate.ec.europa.eu/eu-action/carbon-markets/eu-emissions-trading-system-eu-ets/union-registry_en)
Keeping track of ownership of allowances.
[Ensuring the integrity of the European carbon market](https://climate.ec.europa.eu/eu-action/carbon-markets/eu-emissions-trading-system-eu-ets/ensuring-integrity-european-carbon-market_en)
Preventing market abuse and misconduct.
[International carbon market](https://climate.ec.europa.eu/eu-action/carbon-markets/eu-emissions-trading-system-eu-ets/international-carbon-market_en)
Emissions trading systems around the world.
[Development of EU ETS (2005-2020)](https://climate.ec.europa.eu/eu-action/carbon-markets/eu-emissions-trading-system-eu-ets/development-eu-ets-2005-2020_en)
Set up in 2005, the EU ETS is the world’s first international emissions trading system. It is now in its fourth phase (2021-2030).
[International carbon pricing and markets diplomacy](https://climate.ec.europa.eu/eu-action/carbon-markets/international-carbon-pricing-and-markets-diplomacy_en)
Supporting partner countries to develop effective carbon pricing policies and robust approaches to international carbon markets.
[How do Member States use ETS revenues?](https://climate.ec.europa.eu/eu-action/carbon-markets/eu-emissions-trading-system-eu-ets/how-do-member-states-use-ets-revenues_en)
Explore how ETS revenues are used across the EU
