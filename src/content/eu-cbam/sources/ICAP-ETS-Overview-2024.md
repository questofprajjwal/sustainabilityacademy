#  Welcome to the ICAP ETS Map
Body (teaser + metatags)
The ICAP ETS Map provides and visualizes up-to-date information on ETSs around the world – including systems that are in force, under development and under consideration. The interactive map features downloadable factsheets and gives granular information on individual design aspects.
